展映预告（一）| 瑞士 Fantoche 国际动画电影节影片介绍

Fantoche 是瑞士最负盛名的动画电影节，也是世界上最重要的动画电影活动之一。它每年九月在瑞士的巴登镇举行，为期六天。本次展映由 Fantoche 组委会精选，代表了欧洲独立动画的视角，精选的这些实验动画影片挑战了动画电影的艺术边界，并因其艺术独立性而与众不同。

2022 东布洲国际动画周将展映瑞士 FANTOCHE 国际动画电影节的精选的 10 部作品。

展映时间：2022年11月9日
展映地点：江苏省南通市海门区东布洲动画基地

FANTOCHE 展映影片信息

1. _ImEdge

《_ImEdge》中没有主人公，也没有叙述，只存在令人印象深刻的视觉和听觉事件。在黑色的背景下，白色的线条在景观、深度和运动的诠释中扭曲和转动。最大程度的减少反而创造了一种动态的脉冲。

“_ImEdge” has no protagonist and no narration. Instead: an impressive visual and acoustic event. Against a black background, white lines twist and turn in an interpretation of landscape, depth and motion. Maximum reduction creates a dynamic impulse.

2. Wildebeest

去野生动物园是许多人的梦想。然而对于中年夫妇琳达和特洛伊尔来说，当他们被遗弃在荒野中时，这变成了一场可怕的真实冒险。

Going on a safari is a dream for many. For middle-aged couple Linda and Troyer, it turns into a horribly real adventure when they get left behind in the wilderness.

3. Darwin’s Notebook

从故乡被掠夺的人，他们的回归带来了困难的问题和令人不安的遭遇。当人们的“根“被夺走，他们被置于陌生的文化中，会发生什么？殖民化的影响至今仍可触及。

The return of native people snatched from their homeland raises difficult questions and unsettling encounters. What happens to people when their roots are taken from them and they’re subjected to unfamiliar cultures? The effects of colonisation remain palpable to this day.

4. Fest

一个街区聚会的录像。

Footage from a block party.

5. La Chute

随着外星人降临地球并削减人口，世界秩序崩坏。由这些外星人引发的悲剧性堕落，导致了关键对立面的诞生：地狱和天堂。

As celestial beings descend to Earth vitiating its population, the world’s order unbalances. Initiated by these terms, a tragic fall leads to the parturition of crucial opposites: Hell and Heaven’s circles.

6. Enough

失去自制力的时刻。

Moments of lost self-control.

7. III

一个男人和一个女人在候车室相遇，并立即接近对方。他们开始了一场逐渐变得越来越凶猛的游戏。他们的脸就像面具，而形状慢慢失去了完整性。身体像粘土一样形成，互相拥抱，直到不可能的极限。”III”是一个女人在与一个男人的疲惫关系中的肖像，它同时具有诱惑力和排斥力。这部电影是三联画”不可能的人物和其他故事”的第三部分。

A Man and a Woman meet in a waiting room and immediately get closer to each other. They commence a game that gradually gets more and more ferocious. Their faces resemble masks while shapes slowly lose their integrity. Bodies are formed like clay, embracing each other until the limits of impossibility. “III” is a portrait of a woman in an exhausting relationship with a man, which allures and repulses at the same time. The film is the third part of the triptych “Impossible Figures and other stories”.

8. Anxious Body

生物、人工事物、几何、形状和线条。当这些不同的元素聚集在一起时，一个新的趋势就诞生了。

Living things, artificial things, geometry, shapes and lines – when these different elements come together, a new direction is born.

9. Manivald

马尼瓦尔德是一只33岁的雄性狐狸，他仍然住在家里，尽职尽责地练习钢琴。直到遇到了一只有吸引力的、修理洗衣机的狼，让他看到了爱情。只有一个问题：马尼瓦尔德的母亲也看上了这只狼。

Manivald is a 33-year-old male fox who still lives at home and dutifully practises the piano. That is, until an attractive wolf – who is there to fix the washing machine – opens his eyes to love. There is only one problem: Manivald’s mother has her eyes on the repair-wolf too.

10. Kids

我们到底有多独特？无论我们多么想将自己与他人区分开来，我们都无法逃避群体的动态。这部影片显示，让人们一起做同样的事情是多么简单和迅速。

How unique are we, really? However much we may want to differentiate ourselves from others, we can’t escape the group dynamic. This film shows just how simple and quick it is to get people to do the same thing together.
